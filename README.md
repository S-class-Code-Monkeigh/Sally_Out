# Sally_Out
A simple strategy troop placing, lane management game.

# Download and play here:
(Enter Itch.io link)

# Gameplay Loop
The gameplay loop will consist of 
1. The player entering a level,
2. Getting upgrade currency, 
3. Upgrade troops, 
4. And enter another level, 
5. Repeat. 
-The player can try as many times as he wants if he keeps losing to a level.
-Endless mode for endgame content. points based of time

# Level Gameplay
- Each level has 5 lanes, monsters can spawn in any one of the lanes and the player has to send out troops to counter monsters
- The player must defend their base from monsters that spawn on the right side of the map and can spawn in any of the 5 lanes
- A player can choose to stop units to stack them before allowing them to charge at the enemy.
- The player has a supply bar, which will be the basic economy.
- Troops will have varying costs based of stats and abilities
- The player can use spells to aid his troops

# Progression
- After every level the player will be able to unlock a new troop or spell
- The player can upgrade troops enabling them to "knight" the unit giving the unit a massive increase in stats
